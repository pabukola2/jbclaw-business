🎮 Discord Bot Setup

Get your bot token in 5 minutes

👋

Welcome!

We'll get your Discord bot token in just 5 minutes.

What you'll do:

  1. Go to Discord Developer Portal
  2. Create a new application
  3. Add a bot to it
  4. Copy your token
  5. Paste it back to the setup wizard
🔧

Step 1: Open Developer Portal

Click the link below to open Discord Developer Portal:

🌐
Open Discord Developer Portal

Or go to: discord.com/developers/applications

✨

Step 2: Create Application

In Developer Portal, create a new application for your bot.

📋
Click: "New Application"
Enter your agent's name
Click: "Create"

Example names:

  1. Sales Bot
  2. Support Hero
  3. My AI Assistant
🤖

Step 3: Add Bot

In your application, add a bot.

🎮
Click: "Bot" (left sidebar)
Click: "Add Bot"
Confirm when asked
🔑

Step 4: Copy Bot Token

Now you'll see your bot token.

📋
Under "TOKEN" section
Click: "Copy"
(or click the token to copy it)

Important:

  1. ✅ Keep this token secret
  2. ✅ Don't share it with anyone
  3. ❌ Don't post it online
⚙️

Step 5: Set Permissions

You need to set permissions for your bot.

In Developer Portal:

  1. Click "OAuth2" (left sidebar)
  2. Click "URL Generator"
  3. Under Scopes, check: "bot"
  4. Under Permissions, check: "Send Messages", "Read Messages"
  5. Copy the generated URL at bottom
  6. Open the URL in browser
  7. Select your Discord server
  8. Click "Authorize"

Your bot is now in your Discord server!

✅

Step 6: Paste Your Token

Go back to the setup wizard and paste your token.

When the setup wizard asks:

  1. "Paste your Discord bot token:"
  2. Paste the token you copied
  3. Press Enter
  4. Continue with the wizard

✓ You're Done!

Your bot token is ready. Go back to the setup wizard and continue!
